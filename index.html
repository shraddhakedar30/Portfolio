About

The designer
behind the work.

"I design from the problem outward. Research first. Screens last."

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate finishing an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction at UCD. Originally from Mumbai, I moved to Dublin in 2025 — and immediately started noticing services that needed redesigning.

My work sits at the intersection of research, systems thinking, and design. I'm less interested in making things look good and more interested in making them work better — for the people who have no choice but to use them.

I'm drawn to public sector and civic design because those services affect everyone, and they're often the least designed. The RideSafe project started from watching cyclists navigate unsafe infrastructure on my walk to UCD every morning.
